Music Therapy and Child Enrichment Services

Resonate Music Therapy Studio is the only Music therapy studio in the Visayas located in Dumaguete City. It is a local studio and clinic that provides music therapy services to individuals and groups across the lifespan and welcomes ALL abilities.
