Music Therapy and Child Enrichment Services

Resonate Music Therapy Studio is the only Music therapy studio in the Visayas located in Dumaguete City, Negros Oriental. Our studio offers specialized music therapy services catering to individuals and groups of all ages and abilities. We are committed to providing inclusive support to everyone who walks through our doors. Whether you're seeking therapeutic intervention or simply exploring the healing power of music, we're here to help you on your journey.

What is Music Therapy

"Music Therapy is a clinical and evidenced based-based application of music interventions to accomplish individualized goals within the therapeutic relationship by a credentialed professional who has completed an approved music therapy program."
- American Music Therapy Association
